The Three Little Pigs

Once upon a time, three little pigs set out to build their own homes. 
The first pig quickly built a small house of straw. It was easy and fast, 
but not very strong. The second pig built a house of sticks. It looked 
better than the straw house, but it was still weak. The third pig worked 
hard and built a solid house of bricks, which took more time but was very strong.
